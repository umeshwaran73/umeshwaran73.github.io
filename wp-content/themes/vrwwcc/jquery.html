Welcome to vrwwcc

vrwwcc™ – The Secunderabad Diagnostic & Research Centre is a 35 year old laboratory providing diagnostic services to its clients. The diagnostic centre's mission is "to serve the cause of diagnostic medicine with total dedication and commitment giving top priority to quality in delivering reliable, dependable and repeatable results for effective patient care."

The motto of the organization is "Truth is Life" and its NABL Accreditation reassures its Motto. The laboratory believes in being truthful in the reporting of test results.
