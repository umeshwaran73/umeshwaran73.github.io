About Us

VRWWCC™ : V R Workplace Wellness Care Centre – First Exclusive Medical Centre for workplace wellness care in Chennai providing customized healthcare packages according to the nature of occupation of the worker/workplace.

Started in 2015 by a group of renowned and ambitious doctors , who are experts in the field of occupational health. We are an ISO 15189:2012 certified medical laboratory. We aim to help the clients by providing safe and healthy workplace for the workers and monitor, maintain the health of the workers which in turn increases the productivity of the workers and management.
